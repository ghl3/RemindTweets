RemindTweets

Create and receive reminders using nothing but twitter

How it works

Simply sent a tweet to the handle @@RemindTweets indicating what you want to be reminded of and when you would like to receive your reminder.

@@RemindTweets will send you a tweet at the specified time with your reminder. There are a few types of tweets that can be accepted and converted into reminders: 

@@RemindTweets Remind me to TAKE OUT THE TRASH in 4 HOURS.
@@RemindTweets Remind me to GET MY LAUNDRY TOMRROW at 6:15pm.
@@RemindTweets Remind me to BUY GROCERIES WEDNESDAY at 7:30pm EVERY WEEK.

My Reminders

To see any reminders that you've scheduled, simply visit your reminders page at:

remindtweets.com/user/<TwitterId>/reminders
